Wizard's First Rule

My Thoughts

I absolutely love this book. It is extremely well written and entertaining all the way through.This book makes you feel for the characters like few books I've ever read. It has a nice steady pace throughout the entirety of the book. While a couple parts are predictable, they are predictable for a reason... everyone wants them to happen. On the opposite end, some parts I never would have seen coming. Those were the best parts of the book to me. This book is over 800 pages long and I still didn't want it to end.

“Goodkind choreographs and intricate duality in which the dividing line between good and evil is often turned upside down.” ―Romantic Times -Los Angeles Times Book Review
