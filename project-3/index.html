My Thoughts

I absolutely love this book. It is extremely well written and entertaining all the way through.This book makes you feel for the characters like few books I've ever read. It has a nice steady pace throughout the entirety of the book. While a couple parts are predictable, they are predictable for a reason... everyone wants them to happen. On the opposite end, some parts I never would have seen coming. I still didn't want it to end.
